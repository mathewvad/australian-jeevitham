Who I Am

A first-hand voice, not a travel guide.

I am Binu Vadakkedath, a Malayali living and working in Queensland, Australia. I started the Australian Jeevitham (ഓസ്‌ട്രേലിയൻ ജീവിതം) channel almost by accident, but I kept going because it gives Malayalis who may never get the chance to visit Australia a real, honest look at life here.

Everything I share comes from lived experience: the backyard kitchen garden I keep after work hours, the job search and visa process I went through myself, and the everyday routines of life in regional Queensland. I am not a migration agent, a travel operator, or a government body. I am a resident sharing what I have genuinely seen and done.

Where a topic needs professional advice - visas, healthcare, or legal matters - I say so clearly and point people to the right registered authority, such as a MARA-registered migration agent for visa questions.
