How To Migrate to Australia.

A personal, first-hand look at the paths people take to move here — arriving with a job offer, or lodging a visa independently. ഓസ്ട്രേലിയയിലേക്കു വരാനുള്ള വഴികൾ — ജോലി കിട്ടി വരാം, അല്ലെങ്കിൽ സ്വന്തമായി വിസ അപ്ലൈ ചെയ്യാം.

The Routes

Work visas, self-lodged applications, and what to check first

⚠️

Australian Jeevitham shares personal experiences only and is not a registered migration agent. Migration law changes often and every case is different — always get advice from a MARA-registered migration agent before making decisions about your visa. You can find or verify a registered agent through the Office of the Migration Agents Registration Authority (OMARA).
